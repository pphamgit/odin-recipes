EGG ROLLS

HOMEPAGE | PIZZA | SPAGHETTI

The Vietnamese egg rolls are a variation from Chinese egg rolls... but the Vietnamese ones are way better.

They are made with the following ingredients: (this list is specificially for the egg rolls in the picture above)

  • Ground pork
  • Ground turkey
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Shredded carrots
  • Yellow onion
  • Bean threads/cellophane noodles
  • Sugar
  • Fish sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Cornstarch
  • Garlic powder
  • Eggs
  • Spring roll/egg roll wrappers
